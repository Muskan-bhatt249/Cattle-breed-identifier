Tips for Best Identification

Follow these guidelines to get the most accurate breed identification results

📸

Photo Quality

  • • Upload clear photos from the side angle
  • • Ensure proper lighting — avoid shadows or glare
  • • Capture the full body shape and proportions
🐄

For Buffaloes

  • • Side + head photos improve accuracy
  • • Show distinctive horn shape and size
  • • Include body markings if visible
⚡

Best Results

  • • Use high-resolution images (1MB+)
  • • Avoid blurry or distant shots
  • • Single animal per photo works best
🎯

Additional Info

  • • Include age, milk yield if known
  • • Note any distinctive features
  • • Location/region can help accuracy
